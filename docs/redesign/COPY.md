# Tivnili v2 — Complete Copy

## Copy Principles

**Hebrew first:** All copy written in Hebrew natively, NOT translated from English.

**Yossi's voice:**
- Short sentences (Yossi reads on phone between customers)
- Plain Hebrew a 50-year-old café owner uses
- No SaaS-speak: No "empower," "unlock," "transform"
- Concrete over abstract: "drafts your quotes" not "enhances your workflow"
- Honest: Say uncomfortable truths competitors won't say

**אמת motif:** At least one moment where we say something uncomfortably honest.

**Founding clients framing:** Stated openly, turned into strength.

**Delivered in:**

1. **Hero subhead:** "I'm building my founding client group" — openly states we're just starting, not established

## Translation Notes (Hebrew → English)

The English copy is a **port** of the Hebrew, not a parallel creation. The Hebrew was written first, natively, for Yossi. The English maintains the same tone and structure but is adapted for readability in English (not literal translation).

**Examples where English differs slightly:**

| Hebrew (native) | Literal translation | English port (used) |
|---|---|---|
| "הדברים שאוכלים לך את הזמן" | "the things that eat your time" | "the stuff eating your time" |
| "לא עוד לקוחות שמחליקים בין הסדקים" | "no more customers that slip between the cracks" | "no more customers slipping through the cracks" |
| "בונה סיכום יומי אוטומטי" | "building an automated daily summary" | "building an automated daily brief" |

These are minor adaptations for natural English flow, but the meaning and tone remain identical.
